Our Services

AtlasCraft Construction provides professional remodeling and home improvement services designed to enhance the beauty, functionality, and value of your home.

Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodeling

Upgrade your kitchen with new cabinetry, countertops, backsplashes, lighting, and flooring. We create spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Bathroom Remodeling

Transform outdated bathrooms with modern tile, custom showers, vanities, fixtures, and finishes designed to fit your style and needs.

Flooring Installation

We install luxury vinyl plank, hardwood, laminate, and tile flooring with attention to detail and long-lasting craftsmanship.

Interior Painting

Refresh your home with professional painting services that deliver clean lines, smooth finishes, and lasting results.

General Home Improvements

From trim work and drywall repairs to custom interior upgrades, we provide a wide range of remodeling solutions tailored to your home.

Our Process

1. Consultation

We discuss your goals, ideas, budget, and timeline to understand the vision for your project.

2. Estimate

We provide a detailed estimate outlining the scope of work, materials, and expected costs.

3. Construction

Our team completes the project with quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and clear communication.

4. Final Walkthrough

We review the completed work with you to ensure everything meets your expectations.
